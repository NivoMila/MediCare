Find a Doctor. Save a life.








MediCare is the newest venture in medical technology, and is on a mission to provide easy accessibility of over 10,000 doctors in various specializations, catered for the needs and requirements of elderly people across India.

Our Motto

Technology which is simple enough that can be used by anyone!

Features

  • Extensive database of over 10,000 doctors across the country.

  • Easy to access UI for the benefit of elderly people.

  • Identification of specialized doctors based on required treatment for a specified disease.
